The method

The Repeatable
Thought Process

Great decisions aren't luck — they're a process you can run on every street, in every spot. Here's the loop I'll drill into your game.

1

Assign the range

Before anything else — what hands can they actually have here, given the action so far?

2

Read the dynamics

Who are they, how are they playing tonight, and how do they see you? Adjust accordingly.

3

Size & exploit

Pick the line and the sizing that print the most against that specific range — then repeat.
